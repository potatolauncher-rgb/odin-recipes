Three Cheese Pizza

This simple pizza is budget-friendly and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 8 oz bag of shredded colby and monterey jack cheese
  • 1 8 oz bag of shredded mozerella cheese
  • 1 6 oz can of tomato paste
  • 1 cup of water
  • Italian Seasoning
  • 1 can of generic brand pizza crust

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
  2. Mix can of tomato paste with 1 cup of water, and season with italian seasoning to your preference.
  3. Lay out pizza crust, and spread the tomato paste mixture over it.
  4. Sprinkle cheese over crust liberally, to your liking
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Serve
