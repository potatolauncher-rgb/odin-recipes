Easy Brownies

These brownies are great for when you want a quick dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1/3 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup of oil (whatever you have on hand)
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp of vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
  2. Whisk oil and sugar together until combined.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla and mix until combined.
  4. Add cocoa powder and mix until combined.
  5. Add remaining dry ingredients and mix until combined.
  6. Pour into greased 8x8 pan.
  7. Cook for 20 minutes, and let cool for 10 minutes.
  8. serve
