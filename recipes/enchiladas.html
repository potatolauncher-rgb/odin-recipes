Beef Enchiladas

These enchiladas are delicious comfort food!

Ingredients

  • 1 15 oz can of red enchilada sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small yellow onion, diced
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz lean ground beef
  • 1 tbsp taco seasoning
  • 8 large corn tortillas
  • 8 oz block monterey jack and colby blend cheese, shredded

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Heat oil in large pan over medium heat. Add onion and ground beef and saute until beef is browned.
  3. Put a thin layer of enchilada sauce on bottom of pan, about 1/2 cup.
  4. Place a small amount of the ground beef and onion mixture in your tortilla, along with some of the shredded cheese. Roll and place in pan. Repeat for each tortilla.
  5. Pour the rest of enchilada sauce over top of enchiladas, and sprinkle with cheese to your liking.
  6. Bake uncovered for 20 minutes and serve immediately.
